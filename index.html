❄️ Snow World ❄️

Controls:

  • W A S D - Move around
  • Left Click + Drag - Roll a snowball (grows with distance!)
  • Right Click - Levitate snowball, then click to drop

Goal: Roll 3 snowballs of different sizes and stack them to build a snowman!

Snowballs: 0/3
Drag on the snow to roll a snowball!
